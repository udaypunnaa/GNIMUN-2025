Message from Secretary General

Dear Delegates,

It is with immense pleasure and a deep sense of responsibility that I welcome you to the GNI Model United Nations 2025. Serving as the Secretary General of this edition is a true honor, and I am grateful for the opportunity to lead this conference with the unwavering support of our institution and organizing committee.

This conference is the culmination of dedicated efforts, careful planning, and a shared vision to create a platform where students can engage in meaningful dialogue on pressing global issues. MUNs are more than simulations; they are spaces that encourage diplomacy, critical thinking, and collaboration among young leaders who aspire to make a difference.

We are committed to ensuring that every delegate receives the highest quality experience,one that is academically rigorous, professionally organized, and personally enriching. It is our promise to provide an environment that fosters learning, constructive debate, and mutual respect, while also encouraging innovative solutions to contemporary challenges.

I extend my sincere gratitude to all those who have contributed to making this conference possible and warmly invite each of you to be an active participant in this endeavor. Together, let us uphold the spirit of diplomacy and make this edition of GNIMUN a memorable success.

Yours sincerely,

Krithi Bhaskara
Secretary General
